Directions & What To Catch

  • During the game the object is to catch all of Chipotle's guacamole ingredients. They are cilantro, lime, avacado, salt, jalapeno, and red onion.
  • Crush as many Chipotle guac ingredients as you can to make a batch of the good stuff in order to recieve a discount code. Achieve MVP status by making a full batch in each round. Watch out for incorrect ingredients. Crush one and you'll lose a point.
  • After successful completion of the game, you will be able to enter in your name and contact information for the discount code to be sent to you.
